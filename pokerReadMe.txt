Each card is defined by it's Suit ordinals and Rank
Each column defines one card, first letter defines a suit as:
S - Spades
H - Hearts
D - Diamonds
C - Clubs
and second letter/number(s) defines rank of a card
A - Ace
J - Jack
Q - Queen
K - King
other ranks are defined directly by a number given,
Column 6 defines a clasification of poker hand
0 - Royal Poker
1 - Poker
2 - Four of a kind
3 - Full house
4 - Flush
5 - Straight
6 - Three of a kind
7 - Two pairs
8 - Pair
9 - High card/Nothing
